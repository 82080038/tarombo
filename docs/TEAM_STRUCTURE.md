## LAMPIRAN: JOB DESCRIPTION TEMPLATE

### A. Lead Platform Engineer

```markdown
# Lead Platform Engineer - Tarombo Digital

## Mission
Build scalable, reliable backend systems for complex genealogical data and cultural algorithms.

## Responsibilities
- Design and implement core genealogy APIs
- Build relationship calculation engine (tree/graph algorithms)
- Ensure data integrity and security
- Lead backend team (3-4 engineers)

## Requirements
- 7+ years backend development
- Expert in PostgreSQL, Redis, Graph databases
- Experience with complex data relationships
- Batak cultural knowledge (preferred, not required)

## Nice to Have
- Experience with genealogy/family tree apps
- Knowledge of Indonesian market
- Golang or Rust experience
```

### B. Product Manager (Adat)

```markdown
# Product Manager (Adat Engine) - Tarombo Digital

## Mission
Build the world's first digital platform for Batak kinship systems.

## Responsibilities
- Define and prioritize Dalihan Na Tolu features
- Work with Tetua advisors for cultural validation
- Design partuturan calculation workflows
- Lead Adat Squad (4-5 people)

## Requirements
- 4+ years product management
- Deep understanding of Batak culture (mandatory)
- Experience with complex rule-based systems
- Stakeholder management skills

## Unique Aspect
This role requires direct collaboration with Batak elders (Tetua) and cultural institutions.
```

### C. Tetua Advisor

```markdown
# Tetua Advisor (Part-time) - Tarombo Digital

## Mission
Ensure cultural authenticity and accuracy of Batak kinship features.

## Responsibilities
- Validate adat calculations and workflows
- Review content for cultural sensitivity
- Connect with broader adat community
- Provide guidance on ceremony protocols

## Requirements
- Recognized Tetua Adat or cultural expert
- Deep knowledge of specific sub-suku (Toba/Karo/Simalungun/etc)
- Willingness to engage with technology
- Good communication skills

## Commitment
- 5-10 hours per week
- Honorarium-based (not full-time employee)
```
